I RUN



Away from work I love to run — running is my thing. Before I ran I rode, competitively. I find physical challenge as rewarding as the creative kind and pushing my mind and body ultimately brings out the best in both. I once rode 3,300km (solo!) in 21 days for a personal challenge (raising money for charity along the way). Currently I practice mindfulness, run regularly, drink rarely, drug never, fast intermittently, and Wim Hoff daily.



tl;dw — Wimm Hoff is redefining the science of human physiology through the power of his mind. It’s almost unbelievable (and I don’t use adverbs lightly). Part of Wimm’s daily routine is cold water therapy. It’s also part of mine.
