ABOUT ME



Below is my Tinder profile verbatim. I say if you can’t use your Tinder profile as a sample of your writing then you’re either a weak writer, a liar, or both.

Hi I’m Lane — a halloumi whore and affogato addict who dreams of liberation and llama farming. I’ll love you forever if you’ll just read me aloud DFW’s Infinite Jest… a book I’ve owned forever but never read. If you’re not down for David can we fly to Tokyo, suck down saké, and sing karaoke until sunrise?

Wine and art are the way to my heart when cheese and coffee aren’t appropriate. I’m real and really on Twitter @THINKLN.

Nb: I don’t understand footy, fishing, or photos with tied-up tigers in Thailand.
