About CONCISELY

Hi I’m Lane — aka: CONCISELY. I’m a writer and conceptualist with a gift for delivering clear, creative language that’s easy to read, understand, and engage with. Short-form is my specialty — ads, blurbs, slogans, CTAs, value propositions, etc. — and more in less is my style.

Before I write anything I research everything — the brand, products/services, customers, competitors — so when I write I write real. Using logic, empathy, and art I can craft the most complicated concepts into beautiful, usable, actionable messages… and I don’t use adverbs lightly.
